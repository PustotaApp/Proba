About us

White Star is a young online store of goods for amateur astronomy.

We present a variety of telescope models to choose from. Our models can be suitable not only for novice astronomers, but also for advanced users.

Among the different models, you can find refractor telescopes, reflector telescopes, azimuthal and equatorial mount telescopes. The goals of our telescopes are deep space and objects of the Solar system.

In addition to telescopes, various accessories and accessories are available for sale. For example: replacement lenses, cases, cleaning brushes, tripods, flashlights, maps and globes.

We purchase directly from manufacturers, so we have some of the lowest prices on the market.

Delivery is made throughout the CIS countries, Eastern Europe and Asia. The delivery price depends on the specific city and the transport company.
